Product Description

A durable, quality, interior modified vinl acrylic paint. For use on walls and collings of primed plaster, wallboard, wood, masonary and primed metal. Hiding and sheen are such that application of two coat is sufficient.


Uses

A fast drying latex designed for all interior / exterior applications where high hiding product is required.


Technical Data

Type : Modified vinyl acrylic
Tint Range : As required
Recommended Number of Coats : 2
Theoretical Coverage : 400 sq. feet / 450 sq. feet per US gal @ 4 mils wet
Density : 11.7 ±0.2 lbs/gal
Weight Solids : 52.7 ±0.2%
Volume Solids : 35.9 ±0.2%
VOC : 50 g/L
Specific Gravity : 1.40


Surface Preparation

Surfaces must be clean and dry and free from dust, grease and other contaminants. All surface contamination shall be removed by washing with an appropriate cleaner, rinsed thoroughly and aloowed to dry. Any peeling paint shall be sanded. All glossy surfaces shall be sanded dull. Stains from water, smoke, grease etc shall be sealed with appropriate primer / sealer. Clean all sanding dust as it will decrease adhesion. For drywall fill all cracks and nail holes with a patching compound and sand smooth. Clean all sanding dust. For masonery, concrete, cement and block all surface must be cured as per the supplier's recommendations. All form and release agents must be removed. Rough surfaces can be filled to provide a smooth surface. For plaster surfaces, bare plaster shall be cured and hard. For wood surfaces, any exposed wood shall be sanded to a freshsurface. Use a wood filler or putty to fill all holes / imperfections and sand smooth. Remove all sanding dust.


Application & Clean Up

Brush, roll or Spray.
Touch : 15-30 minutes
Recoat : 4-5 hours
Drying and recoat times are temperature, humidity and film thickness dependent. Tools cleanup with regular warm water.


Miscellaneous Information

If mild due is present, surface should be washed using a bleach solution. Apply solution and scrub area affected. Allow solution to remain for 10 minutes on surface and then rinse thoroughly with water. Allow surface to dry completely before painting.
